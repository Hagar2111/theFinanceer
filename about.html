About the Founder

Hesham Mokhiemer is a Senior Finance Executive, CFO, Head of Finance, and International Accounting and Finance Expert Trainer with more than 20 years of experience in the Middle East and Africa. He has held numerous high-impact leadership positions for prominent companies throughout a variety of sectors, including Diversified Groups, Trading, Engineering, Industrial, Power & Energy, Electrical, Manufacturing, and Retail. Hesham is an International Accounting and Finance Trainer who has organized and taught accounting and finance courses and workshops for more than a decade for some of the leading training companies in the Middle East. He has delivered more than 8,000 hours of workshops and courses to over 10,500 trainees, and he holds an exceptional track record of the number of candidates who have passed CMA exams under his supervision.

Hesham's specialist expertise lies in Financial & Business Planning, Financial Reporting, Planning & Analysis, Budgeting & Forecasting, Treasury Management and Working Capital, Pre-IPO activities, M&A including Due Diligence, Management of AR, AP, and Accounting Operations, CAPEX & OPEX, and leading ERP Systems Implementations. He is a visionary and passionate change agent with a flair for cultivating a high-performance culture through driving business efficiencies and improved operational execution. Hesham has worked with internal boards and senior stakeholders to effectively align and deliver organizational objectives while driving the company strategy and vision.

Hesham is passionate about people, with an extensive history of mentoring, motivating, and leading diverse teams. He holds several certifications, including Financial Modeling and Valuation Analyst, Certified Management Accountant (CMA), Certified Treasury Professional (CTP), Certified Corporate Financial Planning and Analysis Professional (FP&A), and Certified Business Consultant. Hesham has a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Liverpool and is certified in the International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) and International Accounting Standards for Public Sector (IPSAS). He is also certified Credit Risk Analyst from Middlesex University London.
